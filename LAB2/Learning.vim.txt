Q1: How do you change the mode from edit to read-only?
A1: by clicking <Esc> button  

Q2:How do you (a) save the file you are editing, and (b) close the file you're editing?
A2: (a)   :wq and press <Enter>  to save the file which you are editing 
    (b)   :q and press <Enter>  to close the file which you are editing 

Q3:How do you delete (a) letter (b) word (c) sentence?
A3: (a) x and   to delete a letter
    (b) dw  to delete a word
    (c) d$ to delete a sentence

Q4:You're editing the document(in edit mode).Explain how you'll replace one word with another?
A4: click <R> to replace one word with another word
